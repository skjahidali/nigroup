Our Story

Who We Are

NI Group is a forward-thinking real estate development company committed to crafting quality living spaces that inspire lifestyles and transform communities. Founded with a vision to redefine urban living, we have grown into one of Kolkata's most trusted names in real estate.

With a legacy built on trust, innovation, and excellence, we specialize in residential, commercial, and plotted developments across Kolkata and beyond. Every project we undertake is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality craftsmanship and thoughtful design.

From our flagship INVICTA project at Kalikapur, Newtown — to our upcoming portfolio of IQRA, INARA Metro, INAYA Residency, IRA Residency, IRIS, and Paradizia — NI Group continues to shape Kolkata's skyline with purpose and passion.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

A decade of growth, trust, and transforming dreams into addresses across Kolkata.

2013 – Foundation

NI Group Is Born

NI Group was founded by Saikh Ataur Rahaman & MD Mokim Sardar with a shared dream — to bring quality, affordable homes to families across Kolkata.

2016 – First Project

First Residential Project Launched

NI Group delivered its first residential apartments, setting the standard for quality construction and timely handover that defines us today.

2019 – INVICTA

Flagship Project: INVICTA

Launched INVICTA at Kalikapur, Newtown — a landmark project that marked NI Group's position as a premium developer in the Newtown corridor.

2021 – 500 Families

500+ Happy Families Milestone

Crossed 500 happy homeowners — a proud testament to the trust families place in NI Group for the most important purchase of their lives.

2023 – Expansion

Multi-Project Portfolio Launch

Announced 6 upcoming projects — IRIS, IQRA, INAYA Residency, INARA Metro, IRA Residency, and Paradizia — across the Newtown–Hatiara growth belt.

2024 – Digital

AI-Powered Customer Experience

Launched AI concierge and digital-first customer journey tools, making property discovery seamless and transparent for every buyer.

2025+ – Future

NI Group 2.0 — Building at Scale

Expanding across West Bengal with 20+ partner developers, new commercial ventures, and a vision to house 5,000+ families by 2028.
